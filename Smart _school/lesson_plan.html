Lesson Plan

In the realm of academic management, the lesson plan emerges as a pivotal tool for monitoring syllabus progress efficiently. It serves as a roadmap for teachers to navigate through subject content systematically, ensuring comprehensive coverage of curriculum objectives. Each lesson plan delineates what will be taught during a specific class period, empowering educators to manage their instructional time effectively.

Adding Lessons:
  • To incorporate lessons into the system:

    Visit Lesson Plan > Lesson. Specify the Class, Section, Subject Group, and Subject. Input the Lesson Name. To include multiple lessons, simply click the Add More button. Confirm by clicking Save. The added lessons will be displayed in the Lesson List for easy reference.

Integrating Topics:
  • For the inclusion of topics, follow these steps:

    Access Lesson Plan > Topic. Select the relevant Class, Section, Subject Group, and Subject Lesson. Enter the respective Topic Name. Add multiple topics using the Add More option. Validate your selections by clicking Save. The added topics will be visible in the Topic List for organizational clarity.

Creating Lesson Plans:
  • Crafting lesson plans is an essential aspect of academic management:

    Initiate by preparing the Class Timetable via Academics > Class Timetable. Proceed to Lesson Plan > Manage Lesson Plan. Select the desired Teacher from the dropdown menu. The teacher's timetable, sorted in ascending order, will be presented below. To create a lesson plan for any period, simply click the + sign.

Zonitoring Syllabus Status:
  • Efficiently track syllabus status using the following steps:

    Navigate to Lesson Plan > Manage Syllabus Status. Specify the Class, Section, Subject Group, and Subject. Click Search to display available lesson topics. Utilize the toggle button in the Action column to update the status of each topic (complete/incomplete). Note: The completion percentage of lessons and subjects is contingent upon topic statuses.

Accessing Syllabus Status Report:
  • To review subject status comprehensively:

    Head to Reports > Lesson Plan and select Syllabus Status Report. Choose the appropriate Class, Section, and Subject Group. Click Search to access the syllabus status overview.

Reviewing Subject Lesson Plans:
  • For an in-depth analysis of lesson plans:

    Navigate to Reports > Lesson Plan and opt for Subject Lesson Plan Report. Select the Class, Section, Subject Group, and Subject. Click Search to view all teacher-created lesson plans. Detailed information on each lesson plan can be accessed by clicking the View icon in the Action column.

Copying Previous Lessons:
  • Treamline lesson planning by replicating past sessions:

    Visit Lesson Plan > Copy Old Lessons. Specify the Session, Class, Section, Subject Group, and Subject. Click Search to retrieve relevant details. Select desired lessons and topics by checking the corresponding checkboxes. Validate your selections by clicking Save.
