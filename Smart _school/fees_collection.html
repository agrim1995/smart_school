Fee collection

Efficient fee collection and management are vital components of academic administration. The Fees Collection module offers a seamless solution for handling student fees, maintaining fee-related data, and ensuring transparency in financial transactions.

Understanding the Workflow:
The Fees Collection module operates through a structured workflow:

Configuration of Fees Type, Fees Group, Fees Master, and Fees Reminder to establish the fee structure for the current session.Collection of fees, adjustment of fee discounts, and tracking of fees collected by students.

Adding Components:
  1. 1.Fees Type:

    Navigate to. Fees Collection > Fees Type. Enter details such as Name, Fees Code, and Description. Click Save to add the record. Manage records in the Fees Type List, allowing for editing or deletion as needed.

  2. Fees Group:

    Access Fees Collection > Fees Group. Create different fee groups (e.g., installment fees, admission fees) by specifying Name and Description. Save the details and manage the records in the Fees Group List.

  3. Fees Master:

    Go to Fees Collection > Fees Master. Select Fees Group, Fees Type, Due Date, and Amount. Specify Fine Type (Percentage/Fixed amount) and save the record. Manage added records and perform edits or deletions as required.

  4. Fees Discount:

    Navigate to Fees Collection > Fees Discount. Add discount details including name, code, type (percentage or fixed amount), and description. View added records in the Fees Discount List and manage them accordingly.

  5. Managing Collections:
    Collect Fees:

    Access Fees Collection > Collect Fees. Select Class and Section and click Search to display the list of students. Click on Collect Fees in the Action column to view student fee details and collect fees.

  6. Search Fees Payment:

    Go to Fees Collection > Search Fees Payment.Enter the Payment ID and click Search to retrieve payment details.

  7. Search Due Fees:

    Access Fees Collection > Search Due Fees to identify overdue fees. Select the desired fees groups, class, and section, then click Search to view a list of students with outstanding fees.

Reports and Analytics:
  1. Fees Statement Report:

    Navigate to Reports > Finance > Fees Statement. Select Class, Section, and Student to view detailed fee statements.

    Balance Fees Report:

    Access Reports > Finance > Balance Fees Report to check outstanding fees, paid fees, or total fees for selected classes and sections.

  2. Fees Collection Report:

    Visit Reports > Finance > Fees Collection Report to generate reports based on various parameters such as duration, class, section, fees type, etc.

  3. Online Fees Collection Report:

    Access Reports > Finance > Online Fees Collection Report to view records of fees collected through online transactions.

  4. Payroll Report:

    Navigate to Reports > Finance > Payroll Report to access payroll-related financial data.

  5. Daily Collection Report:

    Visit Reports > Finance > Daily Collection Report to review daily fee collection data within specified date ranges.

  6. Balance Fees Report with Remark:

    Access Reports > Finance > Balance Fees Report with Remark to view balance fees along with any remarks associated with each student.

  7. Student Access:

    Students can view their fee details by accessing the Fees section via the student panel. If enabled, they can also make fee payments online through the portal.
