Enquiry Management

The Enquiry Management module assists school administrators in efficiently handling inquiries from prospective students and parents. It streamlines the process of collecting and managing information related to admission enquiries, ensuring a smooth transition from inquiry to enrollment.

Examination Module Workflow:

The examination module encompasses various exam types, including General Purpose (Pass/Fail), School Based Grading System, and College Based Grading System.

Workflow:

The Enquiry Management module simplifies the process of managing admission inquiries by providing a systematic workflow:

  1. Setup Enquiry Management:

    Configure settings and parameters for managing admission enquiries.

  2. Admission Enquiry:

    Record and track inquiries from prospective students and parents regarding admission to the school.

  3. Visitor Book:

    Keep a log of visitors to the school premises, including details such as purpose, meeting with, and duration of visit.

  4. Phone Call Log:

    Maintain a record of incoming and outgoing phone calls, including details like caller's name, phone number, call duration, and purpose of the call.

  5. Postal Dispatch:

    Record outgoing postal items, including details such as recipient, address, and dispatch date.

  6. Postal Receive:

    Record incoming postal items, including details such as sender, address, and receive date.

  7. Complaint Management:

    Manage complaints received from various stakeholders, including students, parents, and staff members, ensuring timely resolution and follow-up.

How to Setup Enquiry Management?

    Navigate to Enquiry Management > Setup Enquiry Management. Add necessary parameters such as Purpose, Complain Type, Source, and Reference data. Enter relevant details and save the record.

    Repeat the process for adding entries for Complain Type, Source, and Reference.

  1. Add Student Admission Enquiry

    Go to Enquiry Management > Admission Enquiry. Select the class for which information is needed. Enter details such as source, inquiry dates, and status to search for admission inquiries. Click the "Add" icon to record a new admission enquiry.

  2. Add Visitor Book

    Visit Enquiry Management > Visitor Book. Click on the "Add" button to open the visitor entry form. Enter visitor details including purpose, meeting with, visitor name, phone number, and visit timings. Save the record to add it to the visitor log.

  3. Add Phone Call Log

    Access Enquiry Management > Phone Call Log. Fill in details such as caller's name, phone number, call date, description, and call type (incoming/outgoing). Click "Save" to add the phone call log entry.

  4. Add Postal Dispatch

    Go to Enquiry Management > Postal Dispatch. Enter details like recipient, reference number, address, and dispatch date. Attach any relevant documents if required and save the dispatch record.

  5. Add Postal Receive

    Visit Enquiry Management > Postal Receive. Enter sender details, reference number, address, and receive date for the incoming postal item. Save the record to add it to the received postal log.

  6. Add Complaint

    Navigate to Enquiry Management > Complaint. Select the complaint type, source, complainant, and provide details such as phone number, date, description, and action taken.

    Attach relevant documents and click "Save" to add the complaint entry.

    By efficiently utilizing the Enquiry Management module, school administrators can streamline the process of handling admission inquiries, visitor logs, phone call records, postal dispatches and receives, and complaints, thereby enhancing overall operational efficiency and stakeholder satisfaction.
